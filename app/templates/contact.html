About AUCode

AUCode is a platform for coding challenges created at our university to help students practice and improve their programming skills. This project is currently in its MVP stage and we welcome feedback and suggestions for improvement.

Our goals include:

  • Providing engaging coding challenges for students of all skill levels
  • Creating a community of programmers who can learn from each other
  • Helping students prepare for technical interviews and competitions
